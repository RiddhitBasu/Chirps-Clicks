About Me

I'm a young wildlife enthusiast and a photographer who cares for conservation and sustainability of this beautiful planet. This website has my blogs talking about my wildlife photography and adventure trips across India and other places too. Apart from sharing the photographs taken by me, I want to sensitize my readers about critical topics of ecological imbalance, sustainability and conservation of the habitats that are depleting due to modernization.
